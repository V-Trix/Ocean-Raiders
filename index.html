Ocean Raiders: Project Levisense

Saving Whales, Saving Oceans

Our Mission

Levisense is a project by Ocean Raiders, created to detect and prevent illegal whaling using smart technology. By protecting whales, we support marine ecosystems, fight climate change, and protect the future of our planet.

Theme: Climate Change

Whales play a vital role in ocean health. They help regulate carbon and nutrient levels, supporting marine life and reducing climate impact.

Our Campaign

We used social media, storytelling, and digital tools to raise awareness among students, teachers, and environmental clubs. We presented our solution – Levisense – through videos, posters, and discussions to help people see whales as essential climate protectors.

Impact

  • 800+ views on campaign posts
  • Support from students, teachers, and NGOs
  • Increased awareness and curiosity among youth
  • Inspired peers to start their own marine projects

Best Moment

Students who never cared about whales started asking questions, doing their own research, and even making whale art. That change in mindset was the most powerful outcome.
